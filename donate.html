How to Donate

At The Free Little Flower Stand, every flower, vase, and jar has a story, and we invite you to be a part of ours. Your donations of glass jars, vases, and flowers help us continue spreading joy in our community, one bouquet at a time.

If you would like to contribute, we would be deeply grateful. Please follow the link below to visit our Facebook page. There, you can connect with us, share in the joy, and coordinate your donation with Alyssa. Whether it’s a single flower or a jar that’s been gathering dust, your gift will be cherished and used to bring a smile to someone’s face.

Thank you for your kindness and for being a part of our journey to make the world a little brighter.
